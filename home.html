THE PRODUCT

What is CASS?

CASS provides an additive safety measure for vehicles. This provides a better experience for daily drivers. Our solution increases the reaction time for drivers in order to greatly reduce the number of rear-end collisions that occur. By allowing for a greater reaction time, drivers are given the opportunity to avoid the collision by noticing the obvious indication of a sudden brake through our product. The daily drivers are the people that will benefit from this product that will provide a more comfortable and safe experience for them. CASS is a novel invention designed to improve on the modern brake light.
