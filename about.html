Our Story

Founded in August 2020 co-founders Hritish and Ethan took their high school capstone project to the next level. When studying safety in vehicles, they both realized that there was one major gap in innovation. Brake lights. Brake lights haven’t changed in principle for decades even though they cause the same issues year after year. Drivers can’t tell how hard the person in front is braking, they have to judge that. To solve this, they created CASS. They believed that CASS was and is a needed product in today’s market where cars just a couple or few years old are neglected in terms of safety features and suffer the consequences on the road. They then got to work creating a new preventative safety feature and have since added a co-founder/ CTO (Matt Silverman), two Software Developers (Alyssa Nguyen & Auston Lin) to the team and an Angel Investor (Sam Nanayakkara).

Now as the team expands, CASS is looking to further expand its team as we embark on a journey to embed our product in every car out there!
